About us

Embracing Jaffna's Agricultural Legacy with Pride.

As CEO of Venkadeshwara Agro Industry Pvt Ltd, I'm proud to lead a company deeply connected to Northern Sri Lanka's agricultural heritage.

Based in Colombo, we showcase Jaffna's finest products, emphasizing rice bran and local resources. Beyond manufacturing, we're committed to preserving culture and benefiting communities. Our products not only nourish the body but also honor Jaffna's rich agricultural legacy.
